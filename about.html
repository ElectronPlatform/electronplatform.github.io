About

Built as an RFID intelligence platform

Electron is designed around devices, knowledge, reports, research and card intelligence working together.

Philosophy

Local first, user controlled

Electron should help users understand RFID technology while keeping their data, reports and feedback choices under their own control.

Direction

Beyond one device

The platform is prepared for multiple RFID devices through a future device layer, capability model and shared command libraries.
